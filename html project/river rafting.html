River Rafting in Uttarakhand

Uttarakhand is renowned for its thrilling river rafting experiences, particularly in Rishikesh, often dubbed the 'Adventure Capital of India.' The Ganges River, with its challenging rapids and stunning scenery, attracts adventure seekers from around the globe. The rafting season typically runs from late September to June, with the best conditions found from March to May. Enthusiasts can choose from various grades of rapids, catering to both beginners and experienced rafters.

Rafting trips in Rishikesh often include safety gear, trained guides, and the chance to combine the experience with camping and bonfires along the riverbanks. Visitors can enjoy breathtaking views of the surrounding hills and lush greenery while navigating through the exhilarating rapids. River rafting in Uttarakhand not only offers an adrenaline rush but also a unique opportunity to connect with nature and explore the beautiful landscapes of the Himalayas.
